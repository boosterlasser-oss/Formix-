# Privacy Policy – FORMIX

**Last updated:** June 16, 2026

## 1. Data Collection
FORMIX collects and stores **only locally** on your device:

- **Workout data**: exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration
- **Nutrition data**: meals, recipes, calories, macros
- **Body metrics**: weight entries (user-entered)
- **App preferences**: training type, module selection, UI settings

**No personal data** (name, email, address, location) is collected by the app.

## 2. Data Storage
All data is stored exclusively in a local **Room database** on your device. No data is sent to external servers. The only network calls made by the app are:

- **Open Food Facts API** (optional): when scanning a barcode, the app sends the barcode number to identify the product
- **Google Drive Backup** (PREMIUM, optional): if enabled, an encrypted backup is stored in your personal Google Drive

## 3. Permissions
- **Camera**: only used for barcode scanning (food labels)
- **Internet**: only for Open Food Facts API and Google Drive (if enabled)
- **Record Audio**: only for optional voice input
- **Notifications**: only for training timer reminders

## 4. Third-Party Services
- **Open Food Facts** (openfoodfacts.org) – public food database
- **Google Drive API** – optional cloud backup (PREMIUM feature)
- **Google Play Billing** – in-app purchases

## 5. Data Deletion
Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data. Google Drive backups must be deleted manually from your Google Drive account.

## 6. Contact
For privacy questions: formix.app@email.com

---

**Summary:** FORMIX works offline-first. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backup.
